About Us

Who We Are

STARLINK DATA SERVICES LTD is a registered Internet Service Provider and ICT solutions company headquartered in Bosso, Niger State, Nigeria. We deliver enterprise-grade connectivity and technology services to individuals, businesses, institutions, and communities.

Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality internet and telecommunications services that promote digital connectivity, communication, innovation, and economic development across Nigeria.
